Creating Tomorrow

I am a junior in the College of Computing and Informatics at the University of North Carolina at Charlotte. My plan is to graduate in the Spring of 2014. My goal is to graduate with a B.A. in Software Engineering with a minor in Cognitive Science, and a year later to complete a Masters Degree in Security. I am currently participating in the Student Leadership Corps (SLC) program with the Students and Technology in Academia, Research and Service (STARS), giving me the opportunity to participate in outreach programs with local schools and engage in research. Recently, I started to participate in the Research Experiences for Undergraduates (REU), providing me with the opportunity to work in GameChanger: promoting health and wellbeing with games. This research was funded by the Computer Research Association's Committee on the Status of Women in Computing Research (CRA-W).
