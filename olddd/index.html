Creating Tomorrow

I am a rising senior in the College of Computing and Informatics at the University of North Carolina at Charlotte. Currently, I am participating of the Student Leadership Corps (SLC) with the Students and Technology in Academia, Research and Service (STARS), a program designed to build Leadership skills and participate in service. Since August 2012, I am the coordinator of Students in Programming, Robotics and Computer Science (SPARCS), an outreach program in the Charlotte area aimed to involve k-12 students in Computer Science. During the school year of 2012 - 2013, I was the Evaluation Assistant at UNC Charlotte an Elite group of STARS Leadership Corps students. I have participated in Research Experiences for Undergraduates (REU) with Exergames: 'Promoting Health and Well-being with Games". This research was funded by the Computer Research Association's Committee on the Status of Women in Computing Research (CRA-W).

On August 2013, I was Awarded with the "Outstanding Corps Student Award", an award that is given to only one student from 49 different universities in the United States. This year I was competing with a total of 250 applicants.
