Human-Computer Interfaces


ITIS 3130


In order to register, a student needs to follow different steps. Part of the requirements are making an appointment and meeting with an advisor, signing for classes that are part of the degree path, ensure the prerequisites are being met, and making sure that there is no conflict of schedule between classes. Students also encounter non-required problems when trying to register, such as conflict of schedule in cases where the student have a part-time or full-time job, trying to get in same classes with friends, avoiding certain professors, among others. Comparing problems that students experience and discussing how appealing these ideas were for our group, we concentrated on the different tasks that students have to go through in order to register for classes every semester.
